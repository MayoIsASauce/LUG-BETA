In order to ensure the best possible downloading experience of the LUG Client, refer to the following instructions:

1. Download LUG_installer.exe

2. Run the installer.
2a. If you get a warning prompt, click show more and then press run anyways.
2b. This shows up due to the installer executable not being signed and needing permissions to download files to the device.

3. Choose download options and begin the download.

4. Wait for the installer to finish downloading.

5. Run the game
